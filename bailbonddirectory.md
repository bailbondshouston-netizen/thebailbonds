# Houston-Area Jail & Court Directory

Addresses and phone numbers for the main jails and courts across the Houston metro.
**Always confirm current details** — facilities move, numbers change, and hours
vary. Compiled 2026; verify before you rely on any entry.

> **Need help posting bail?** If a loved one is held at one of these facilities,
> [a licensed Houston bail bondsman](https://bailbondshouston.net/service-areas/harris-county-bail-bonds/)
> can post the bond at any Harris County jail, day or night.
